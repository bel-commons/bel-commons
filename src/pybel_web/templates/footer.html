About

PyBEL Web is developed and maintained by Charles Tapley Hoyt and Daniel Domingo-Fernandez at Fraunhofer SCAI with support from the IMI projects, AETIONOMY and PHAGO. It is built on top of the open source project, PyBEL, whose source code is versioned on GitHub, distributed on PyPI, and documented on Read the Docs. Please feel free to contact us here to give us feedback or report any issues.
