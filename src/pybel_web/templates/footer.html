About

PyBEL Web is developed and maintained by Charles Tapley Hoyt and Daniel Domingo-Fernandez at Fraunhofer SCAI with support from the IMI projects, AETIONOMY and PHAGO. It is built on top of the open source project, PyBEL. Please feel free to contact us here to give us feedback or report any issues.

This is the October 2017 Beta 1 release, meaning some features aren't yet complete.
