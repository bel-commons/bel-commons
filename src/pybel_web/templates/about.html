About

PyBEL Web is developed and maintained by Charles Tapley Hoyt and Daniel Domingo-Fernandez at Fraunhofer SCAI with partial support from the IMI projects, AETIONOMY and PHAGO. It is built on top of the open source project, PyBEL. Please feel free to contact us here to give us feedback or report any issues.

If you use PyBEL Web, please cite us:

  • Hoyt, et al.; PyBEL: a Computational Framework for Biological Expression Language, Bioinformatics, btx660.

Support

This application was developed at the Fraunhofer SCAI Department of Bioinformatics.
The IMI is an EU public-private partnership funding health research and innovation.
AETIONOMY is a project aimed to develop a mechanism-base taxonomy of Alzheimer's disease and Parkinson's disease in order to support stratifying patients.
PhAGO is a project aimed to understand the relation between TREM2, CD33, and microglial activation in the context of Alzheimer's disease.
