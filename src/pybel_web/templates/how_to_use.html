How to Use PyBEL Web

Typical Workflow

Step 1: Upload a BEL Document

Click "Parse BEL", Choose a File, then Click "Upload".

Step 2: Summarize a BEL Document

Click "List Networks" and find your network. Click "Summary".

Step 3: Build a Query

Choose the universe of BEL documents to start with, seed your network, and select filters/mutators.

Step 4: Explore a Knowledge Assembly

From the query builder, click "View my Network" and begin to interact with the explorer.

The explorer contains functions to build further queries.

Step 5: Analyze a Knowledge Assembly

Data sets like differential gene expression can be used to quantify the perturbation amplitude of biological processes in a network using an randomized algorithm based on NPA.
